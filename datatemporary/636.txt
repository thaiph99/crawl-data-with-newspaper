Car imports from China increase sixfold
https://e.vnexpress.net/news/business/economy/vn-index-surges-to-new-peak-4264021.html
Despite the surging number of cars imported from China, the country was the third largest car supplier of Vietnam, after Thailand and Indonesia.. . Up to 80 percent of the completely built-up (CBU) cars imported to Vietnam in Q1 were from Thailand and Indonesia. The number of imported cars from Thailand was 19,300 units, up 56 percent year-on-year, while those from Indonesia stood at 8,950 units, down 26 percent year-on-year.
